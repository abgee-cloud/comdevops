# BCCP 2026–2035 Community Consultations Underway

The Department of Community Development, in partnership with the ABG Department of Personnel and Industrial Relations, has commenced community consultations for the **Bougainville Civil Control Policy (BCCP) 2026–2035**.

## About the BCCP

The BCCP is a strategic policy framework governing civil control and community safety across the Autonomous Region of Bougainville. The 2026–2035 edition is being developed through a participatory consultation process grounded in the **Delaforce Policy Model** and **Chrisinger's SCIO framework**.

## Consultation Schedule

Consultations are being conducted across all 13 districts, with sessions targeting key stakeholder groups including:

- District administrators and village councillors
- Women's groups and youth representatives
- Civil society organisations and faith-based groups
- Security sector partners

## IS&CT Support

The IS&CT Unit is providing digital data collection tools, KoboToolbox-based registration forms, and real-time data dashboards to support the consultation process.
